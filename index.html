Goodnight, Sweet Dreams!

May your night be calm and peaceful, and your dreams be filled with happiness.

Beneath the moon's soft silvery glow,
A gentle breeze begins to flow.
Stars above whisper and shine,
"Goodnight, Biswabir, sleep divine."

Asim, your name a melody sweet,
In dreams, let joy and peace meet.
May stars guide your heart tonight,
Wrapping you in their tender light.
